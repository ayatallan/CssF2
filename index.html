HTML Form Elements

Let's create a form to apply for a trip at a tourism company.

We will collect the following information:

  • Name of the person applying
  • Their social security number
  • Their email address
  • Destination
  • Departure date
  • Return date
  • Their gender
  • An image of their passport
  • In how many stars of an hotel they'd like to stay
  • Any notes they might have
  • The option to include breakfast and lunch
Key takeaways:
  • How can we label our inputs?
  • How can further organize our form by grouping similar inputs?
  • Can we show how many stars the user has selected?
  • How can we submit our form
  • How can mark some of the inputs as required?
  • Is it possible to change the submittion method?
  • How can we reset our form?
  • What other types does the input element support?
